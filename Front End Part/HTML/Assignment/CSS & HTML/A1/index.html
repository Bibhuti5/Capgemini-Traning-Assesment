Who is She ?

Meredith Grey, M.D., F.A.C.S. is a fictional character from the medical drama television series Grey's Anatomy,
which airs on the American Broadcasting Company in the United States.
Meredith Grey is the head of general surgery at Grey Sloan Memorial Hospital. Meredith has always had a lot of high expectations and responsibility riding on her shoulders.


Some Famous Quotes by her are :

  • Time waits for no man. Time heals all wounds. All any of us want, is more time. Time to stand up. Time to grow up. Time to let go.
  • Adapt or die. As many times as we’ve heard it, the lesson doesn’t get easier. Problem is, we’re human. We want more than just to survive. We want success. We want to be the best we can be so we fight like hell to get those things. Anything else feels like death.
  • Just put one foot in front of other.Just get through the day.
  • Sometimes it's good to be scared.It means you still have something to loose.
  • It's important to tell the people you love how much you love them while they can hear you.
  • We'll try and we'll fail again because that's what progress looks like a bunch of failures.
